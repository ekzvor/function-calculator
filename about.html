üí° About the Project

This function calculator was created as an educational project in mathematical analysis. The idea came up during my studies of derivatives in high school, when I realized that most tools online are overly complex or available only in English. There are almost no Ukrainian-language equivalents that provide actual explanation of critical points, extrema, monotonic intervals, etc.

Derivatives are a key topic studied in school throughout the academic year and beyond. This calculator not only performs calculations, but also helps understand structure, logic and graphical behavior of functions.
